Hi!

My name is Angelika and I'm a web developer located in Berlin, Germany.

I currently work for Steady Media, where I help build a membership platform for independent content creators.

I like helping people learn. I mentor Elixir learners on Exercism.io and I'm open for other similar opportunities.

I write this blog to document and share my experiences with various challenges that I encounter writing software. I am by no means an expert, so please take my posts with a healthy dose of skepticism, and make sure to leave a comment if you have constructive feedback.

Personal
